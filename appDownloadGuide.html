How to Download TrashApps Shortcut

Google Chrome

1. Open the web page in the Chrome browser.
2. Click the menu button (three dots) in the top right corner.
3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Create shortcut".
4. Enter a name and click "Add".
5. You can now use it like an app from your home screen.

Safari

1. Open the web page in the Safari browser.
